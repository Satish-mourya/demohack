We Are EDUPATH

We are dedicated to enhancing the educational experience by providing a user-friendly platform for instructors and learners. Our solution simplifies the creation of personalized learning paths, integrates diverse educational resources, and offers real-time progress tracking.

Committed to following UX principles, we aim to make learning efficient, engaging, and accessible for everyone.

Why Choose Us

Simplified Setup

Our platform allows instructors to easily input publication records through a single .bibtex file or an Excel sheet, making it quick and straightforward to create comprehensive learning paths without the hassle of complex data entry.

Real-Time Tracking

Our interactive dashboard provides real-time insights into learners' progress, showing detailed reading statistics, time spent on specific topics, and the total time required to complete a skill. This enables both instructors and learners to stay informed and make data-driven decisions.

Personalized Learning

Our platform allows for the creation of tailored learning paths that cater to individual learner needs. This personalization enhances the effectiveness of the educational process, ensuring that each learner can achieve their specific goals at their own pace.

User-Friendly Design

We prioritize user experience, ensuring that our platform is intuitive and easy to navigate. By adhering to UX principles, we create an environment where both instructors and learners can focus on education without being hindered by complex interfaces.
